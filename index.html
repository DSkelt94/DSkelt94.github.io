Introduction

Welcome to my final year portfolio. This document records the journey of developing a prototype solution in robotic modelling and drone skin design, moving from virtual concepts to physical artefacts using a range of advanced technologies.

This phase includes initial research, brainstorming, and theoretical design. Early sketches and reference models guided the intended direction of the final prototype.
